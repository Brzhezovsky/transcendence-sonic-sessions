Transcendence Sonic Sessions

Transcendence Sonic Sessions is a monthly radio show by Brzhezovsky on KURS Radio featuring Progressive House, Neo Trance, UK Garage and modern underground electronic music.

The project explores contemporary electronic music inspired by the cultural code of underground club scenes from the 1990s and 2000s.
